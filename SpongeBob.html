SpongeBob

Descriptioon

  1. The initial canvas is a picture of the world under the sea.

  2. Click the buttons below the canvas to draw SpongeBob with different emotions.

  3. Click the area that near SpongeBob's mouth to draw a beard for him. Click again to erase the beard.

  4. Click on one of his eyes will close it, and click again will open it.

  5. The effects made by clicking the facial features after clicking the buttons will not disappear.

  6. Refresh the page to reset the canvas.
